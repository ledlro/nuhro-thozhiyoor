Exploring the Heritage & Legacy

Nuhro Thozhiyoor is a non-official, independent digital archive initiative dedicated to documenting the history, legacy, and ecclesial heritage of the Malabar Independent Syrian Church – Thozhiyoor.

About Nuhro

About Nuhro Thozhiyoor

Meaning of Nuhro

Nuhro means Light in Syriac — symbolising illumination of memory, history, and faith.

Nature

This is not an official website of the Malabar Independent Syrian Church. It is an independent heritage and research initiative.

Mission

Preserving ecclesial memory, manuscripts, traditions, and historical narratives using modern digital design.
